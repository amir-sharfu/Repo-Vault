## What is Beehiiv? (1 Sentence)

Beehiiv is an **all-in-one newsletter platform** — writing, website builder, growth tools, monetization, and analytics in one place, built specifically for creators who want to scale.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Before Beehiiv, newsletter creators had to stitch together 5-6 separate tools:

| What You Need | Separate Tool Before |
|---------------|---------------------|
| Email sending | Mailchimp / ConvertKit |
| Website | WordPress / Webflow |
| Referral growth | SparkLoop |
| Ad monetization | Manual brand deals |
| Analytics | Google Analytics |

**With Beehiiv**: All of this in one platform, with growth built in from day one.
