## What is Blotato? (1 Sentence)

Blotato is an **all-in-one AI content engine** — take one piece of content (video, article, PDF) and automatically remix it into posts for LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and more.

---

## The Problem It Solves

You write one blog post. Now you need versions for every platform:

| Platform | Format | Tone | Length |
|----------|--------|------|--------|
| LinkedIn | Article/post | Professional | Long |
| Twitter/X | Thread | Punchy | Short |
| Instagram | Caption | Casual | Medium |
| TikTok | Script | Entertaining | Short |
| YouTube | Description | Informative | Long |

Doing this manually = hours of work. **Blotato does it automatically.**

---

## Real Example

Take 3 YouTube videos → Blotato outputs:
- 1,500-word blog post
- LinkedIn post
- Tweet thread

All in one operation. The AI understands each platform's conventions.
