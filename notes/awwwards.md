## What is Awwwards? (1 Sentence)

Awwwards are the Website Awards that recognize and promote the talent and effort of the best developers, designers, and web agencies in the world.

---

## Award Tiers

| Award | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Site of the Day (SOTD)** | Highest daily honor — excellence in design, UX, and development |
| **Honorable Mention** | Strong design recognition, just below SOTD |
| **Mobile Excellence** | Outstanding mobile-focused design |
| **Developer Award** | Technical excellence in development |
| **Site of the Month** | Best SOTD winner of the month |
| **Site of the Year** | Ultimate recognition — best of the entire year |

| Criteria | Weight |

## How It Works

1. **Submit** your website to Awwwards
2. **Expert jury** evaluates the submission
3. **Community votes** during a 5-day open period
4. **Scored** across all criteria above
5. **Award granted** with global recognition
6. **Featured** in the annual *"365 Best Websites"* book
